# Code of Conduct

Codex Pet Meter uses a simple conduct standard for project spaces.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about risks.
- Keep feedback focused on the code, docs, security model, or user experience.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, sexualized language, or discriminatory
  remarks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing credentials, tokens, logs, or screenshots that expose secrets.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer asks to stop.
- Threats, intimidation, or abusive language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, or block accounts
that make the project unsafe or unproductive. Security-sensitive material may be
removed immediately to protect users.
